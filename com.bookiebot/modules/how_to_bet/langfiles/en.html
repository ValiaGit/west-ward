There are few steps to do before you can make Bets:

  • Register Account from here
  • Confirm Email address
  • Deposit Money with Ecopayz
  • Choose desired Event and Outcome
  • Make bet on Back(For) or Lay(Against) for desired outcome

After you make bet, it's then part of betting exchange system and is available for other users to bet against.
If no one will bet against your bet, and bet stays unmatched after event is finished, your money will be deposited back on your account with not cut commission.
Your bets can be match partly or fully. Unmatched amounts are automatically deposited on users account after event is finished.
You can cancel unmatched amount any time.
If you make Lay bet and win you are charged with 3% commission from your profit. If you make Back bet and win you will be charged with 5% from your profit.
